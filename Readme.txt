STEP 1: ADD PRODUCT
- Click "Add Product"
- Enter name, price, stock

STEP 2: ADD CUSTOMER
- Click "Add Customer"
- Enter name and phone

STEP 3: CREATE INVOICE
- Click "Create Invoice"
- Select customer
- Select product
- Enter quantity
- Click "Add to Cart"
- Click "Save Invoice"

NOTE:
- Stock automatically decreases after invoice
- If stock is low, system will warn you

STEP 4: VIEW STOCK
- Click "Stock View"

STEP 5: VIEW INVOICES
- Click "Invoice History"

PDF:
- Invoice PDF automatically generated
- Saved in same folder
